THE BEGINNING

The Enfield Cycle Company made motorcycles, bicycles, lawnmowers and stationary engines under the name Royal Enfield out of its works based at Redditch, Worcestershire. The legacy of weapons manufacture is reflected in the logo comprising the cannon, and the motto "Made like a gun". Use of the brand name Royal Enfield was licensed by the Crown in 1890.

ROYAL ENFIELD BIKES

See where Royal Enfield motorcycles are still made the old fashioned way! Handcrafted in Chennai. Royal Enfield is amongst the world's oldest motorcycle manufacturers still in business. The Bullet 350 has also been the longest running motorcycle model in continuous production! First built in Great Britain, the Bullet 350's have been made in India at this factory since 1955. Royal Enfield now conducts factory tours for all enthusiasts, seeking to soak in the ambience of the factory that still manufactures the Royal Enfield Bullet 350 and the Classic along with other models.
